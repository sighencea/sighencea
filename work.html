Abrax

A workflow platform for property and maintenance operations.

[THE PROBLEM]

Teams were relying on multiple disconnected tools, leading to missed tasks, no clear ownership, and no reliable audit trail. Property maintenance across dozens of locations was falling through the cracks.

[SYSTEM DECISION]

Design a single task lifecycle with clear states, ownership, and history — removing reliance on memory and informal communication. Every task has an audit trail and a next scheduled moment.

[OUTCOME]

  • • No silent failures
  • • Clear accountability
  • • Scalable workflows
  • • Reduced operational friction

Sinest

A family operations system for reducing cognitive overload.

[THE PROBLEM]

Overwhelmed parents drowning in household chaos. Calendars, chores, medical needs, routines — all invisible "mental load" carried by one person. Tasks become silent burdens instead of shared responsibilities.

[SYSTEM DECISION]

Build a family coordination platform that makes mental load visible. Tasks become shared responsibilities with clear ownership. In homes, just like in code, entropy is the enemy.

[OUTCOME]

  • • Families function like teams, not emergencies
  • • Mental load becomes observable
  • • Clear task ownership and distribution
  • • Reduced cognitive overload
